Crystal Collector

During your travels, you find yourself wandering on an unfamiliar road, driven by an unknown force. This compelling feeling leads you to a cart that is setup in the woods. You see a few gypsies sitting behind a stand and below them on the table are four glowing crystals.

"Ah, traveler, ye' look weary, why don't ya take a seat over and rest ya self and ya horse."

Still compelled by this unseen force, you and your horse do as they say.

"While you're 'ere, ya might as well test ya luck. These fo' crystals will represent fo' random numbas, ya self or us will not know these numbas, but by touching them and seein' them adds up, ya might put togetha' what they are by themselves. Match the crystal amounts to the random numba' we give ya and win double the amount you put down. 'Ow a bout et den?"
